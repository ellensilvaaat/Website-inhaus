## 1.Imagine Your Dream Home

***Must-haves*** – There are the non-negotiable features and elements that you absolutely require in your custom home. Consider the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, layout preferences, specific materials, and any unique architectural elements.

***Nice-to-haves*** – These are the features you would like to include in your home, but they’re not essential. Examples might be a home theatre, a swimming pool, or an outdoor kitchen.

**Here are some tips on how to create a list of requirements:**

### # Evaluate your Lifestyle

Consider how you and your family live and what features would enhance your daily lives. For example, if you enjoy cooking, a large kitchen with high-end appliances may be a must-have.

### # Prioritise your Needs

Identify your non-negotiable requirements, such as the number of bedrooms, bathrooms, or a specific location. These are your must-haves.

### # Consider your Future Needs

Think about your future needs, such as expanding your family, working from home, or ageing in place. These should also be considered in your list.

### # Research Popular Trends

Look at current design and construction trends to find inspiration for your custom-built home. While these trends should not dictate your list, they can offer guidance for potential features to include in your must-haves or nice-to-haves.

## # Search for Custom Home Builders

With your list ready, begin looking for the perfect builder. Use online resources, local directories, and personal recommendations to create a list of promising candidates.

**Look for builders like Inhaus Living** who have a great reputation, an impressive portfolio, and happy clients to make sure your dream home is in good hands. **Here are some of the most effective methods**:

### # Online Searches

The internet is a great resource for finding builders in Sydney. You can use search engines and online directories to find builder websites, read reviews, and compare prices and services.

### # Word of Mouth Recommendations

Ask friends, family, and colleagues for recommendations on builders they have used in Sydney. Positive recommendations can provide valuable insights into the builder’s workmanship, professionalism, and reliability.

### # Industry Associations

Industry associations like the HIA (Housing Industry Association) and MBA (Master Builders Association) can provide you with a list of licensed builders in Sydney who meet their standards and codes of practice.

### # Local Newspaper and Magazines

Check local publications for advertisements and articles featuring builders in Sydney. These can provide you with valuable information about local builders.

### # Home Shows and Expos

Attending home shows and expos in Sydney is an excellent opportunity to meet builders, ask questions, compare services and prices, and find a builder that meets your needs.

## # Check Credentials, Experience, and Portfolio

Review your list and check each builder’s credentials, experience, and portfolio. Make sure they have the required licence and insurance. Look at their past projects, awards, and testimonials to understand their expertise and ability to build the home you want.

**Checking all of these is essential when choosing a custom home builder in Sydney. Here’s why:**

### # Credentials

A reputable builder in Sydney should have the necessary licences, insurance, and permits to comply with regulations. You’ll want to hire someone with an NSW builder’s license. You can check a builder’s credentials through industry associations or government websites.

### # Experience

A builder’s experience is a crucial factor when choosing a custom home builder. More experience means a higher likelihood of successful project delivery and establishment of relationships with suppliers, contractors, and designers.

### # Portfolio

A builder’s portfolio displays their previous work and can give you an idea of their style and quality of work. You can ask for references from previous clients to further assess the builder’s compatibility with your vision.

## # Meet the Builder and Ask Questions

Meet with your top choices to see their communication style, professionalism, and willingness to meet your unique needs. Prepare a list of questions about their experience with custom homes, project management, and estimated timelines. This will help you find a builder who shares your vision and goals.

Here’s a checklist of questions to ask during the interview process:

- How long have you been in business?
- Are you licensed and insured in Sydney?
- Have you worked on similar projects before?
- Can you provide references from previous clients?
- Can you provide a detailed estimate of the project cost as well as the timeline?
- What is your communication process with clients during the building process?
- Do you have an in-house design team, or do you work with external designers?
- How do you handle changes to the project scope or unexpected issues during construction?
- What is your payment schedule and process?

## # Compare and Review Quotes

Get detailed quotes from your top choices and review the costs, including materials, labour, permits, and taxes. Make sure there are no hidden charges or unclear details in the quote. When comparing quotes, remember that the cheapest option might not always be the best. **Consider the builder’s reputation, experience, and your connection with them when deciding**.
